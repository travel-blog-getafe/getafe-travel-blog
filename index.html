Welcome to Getafe

Getafe is a beautiful coastal municipality located in northern Bohol, Philippines. Known for its calm beaches, fishing communities, island adventures, and rich marine biodiversity, Getafe is one of the hidden gems of Bohol.


It serves as a gateway between Cebu and Bohol and offers relaxing scenery perfect for nature lovers and travelers looking for peaceful destinations.

Tourist Spots

Pandanon Island

Pandanon Island is famous for its white sandbar, crystal-clear waters, and relaxing tropical scenery. It is perfect for swimming, island hopping, and beach adventures.

Getafe Coastal Area

Enjoy peaceful coastal views, fishing boats, fresh seafood, and the relaxing atmosphere of Getafe’s shoreline.

Historic Getafe Church

The church reflects the municipality’s rich culture, history, and religious traditions with its beautiful architecture.

Culture and Traditions

The people of Getafe are known for their hospitality, fishing traditions, and strong community spirit. Seafood plays an important role in their local lifestyle and economy.


Festivals, religious celebrations, and local traditions continue to preserve the rich culture and heritage of the municipality.


Getafe is also connected to the famous Danajon Double Barrier Reef, making marine conservation and fishing important parts of local life.
